HOME

Welcome to Marlene's Sweets! Marlene's Sweets is a small business selling delicious desserts from chocolate covered pretzels to flan.
Check out the full menu and order by direct messaging Marlene. (Custom orders are accepted!)

Contact and Order

Follow Marlene on Instagram @marlenes_sweets

Direct message Marlene through her Instagram account and she will take your order. Give her your order and let her know if you have allergies to any nuts if you are ordering a breakable heart.
You do not need to follow Marlene to order, but any support is welcome and appreciated.
Orders will be ready by the date and time you set, same day orders are accepted as long as there is enough time to complete the order.
If you want a custom order use a reference picture and Marlene will discuss with you if it is possible.
Orders are available for pickup at Marlene's address, if you need a delivery that is fine, but there is a small delivery fee.
Accepted payment methods are cash, Venmo, and Zelle.

COVID-19

Everything is made COVID safe. Marlene wears a mask and gloves the entire time she makes your order.
She disinfects the work space before and after making an order.
Curbside pickup is available if you pay beforehand on Venmo or Zelle.
If you pay with cash Marlene will come to the car and wear a mask. You also need to wear a mask.
